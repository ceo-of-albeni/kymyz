WHAT IS KYMYZ?

The national drink of Kazakh and Kyrgyz people and considered as a valuable drink. The drink is made by fermenting a mare milk, it contains a low amount of alcohol. Also, it is used as a medicine for the sick. First, mare milk is poured into the gourd and left to be fermented later. Every fresh portion of milk is added to the rest, then, mixed with a stick. There are several types og the kymyz: freezed kymyz, saamal (fresh milk) kymyz, etc.
